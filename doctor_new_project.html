Why choose us?

With a legacy of more than five decades, Seethapathy Clinic & Hospital in Chennai, is a much sought-after, focussed sub-specialty Hospital with its core in General Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Paediatrics and Family Practice. To complete the spectrum of care provided, the Hospital also provides an Endocrine Clinic and a sub-specialty Urogynaecology Clinic.
